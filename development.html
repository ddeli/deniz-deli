Backup Metadata Analyzer

Development of a web application in collaboration with the University of Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU), the Technical University of Berlin (TU Berlin) and the industrial partner SEP. The aim of the app is to analyze backup metadata for the early detection of anomalies and potential threats.

Involved as Full Stack Web Developer

Secobo

Enjoy Security, with Secobo!

Your SaaS tool for better information security management.

Secobo® supports you in the development and implementation of security guidelines and procedures. It also helps you to monitor, assess risks and manage deviations. Use proven templates and customise them to your needs to save effort and be optimally prepared for threats and audits.

Involved as Product Owner and Software Tester (QA)
